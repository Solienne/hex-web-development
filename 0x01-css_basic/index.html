Studying at Holberton School

Have you ever heard about Holberton School?Well, let me tell you. It is
a college where you can learn about technology and even get a degree for
free (you just need to make contributions after you get stabilished in the
area - to guarentee that others will have the same opportunity you did!).

Me at Holberton School: How it started

It happened suddely. I was on instagram when I saw a publication about
a partnership between Pearson Next and Holberton School and that they were
offering a free course for at least 100 luck folks, once they passed the tests,
a scholarchip to a Full Stack course. Spoiler alert: I was one of them!

How it is going for now

I have to say that these guys take education very seriously. Each one of the 80
projects are very challenging, but they give us what we need to learn and to develop ourselves into full stackers.
